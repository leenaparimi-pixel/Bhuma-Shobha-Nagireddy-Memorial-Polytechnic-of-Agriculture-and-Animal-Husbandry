Agriculture Polytechnic

Bhuma Shobha Nagireddy Memorial Polytechnic of Agriculture, Allagadda, has been started in the year 2017 by Late Sri Bhuma Nagireddy garu (Member of Parliament). BSNRM College has obtained good recognition, for quality and innovative education. It is affiliated to Acharya N.G. Ranga Agricultural University,Lam,Guntur. The college offers course in Diploma in Agriculture with an intake of 40+4= 44 seats.


Animal Husbandry Polytechnic

Bhuma Shobha Nagireddy Memorial Polytechnic of Animal husbandry, has been started in the year 2019 by Late Sri Bhuma Nagireddy garu (Member of Parliament).Animal Husbandry Polytechnic is affiliated to Sri Venkateswara Veterinary University, Tirupati. The college offers course in Diploma in Animal Husbandry with an intake of 50+5= 55 seats.

Admission Process

Eligibility

Candidates must have passed SSC (from AP Board, CBSE, ICSE, or APOSS) or equivalent.
Age Limit: 15 to 22 years

Selection Criterion

Selection is strictly based on the merit of marks obtained in the SSC or equivalent exam.

Seat Allotment

Provisional merit lists are released based on 10th-class marks.Selected candidates are called for counseling and document verification.Original documents, including caste certificates, proof of rural background, and local status, must be submitted.

Fee Structure

Fee Reimbursement provided by the Govt of Andhra Pradesh
